Comprehensive review by departmental selection committees.

5. Notification

Applicants notified of admission decision via email and postal mail.

6. Acceptance & Registration

Accepted students confirm enrollment and complete registration.

Student Transfer Policy

Grand Gedeh University accepts transfer students from other accredited institutions under specific conditions:

Transfer Requirements:
  • Good academic standing at previous institution (minimum GPA 2.5)
  • Official transcripts from all previous institutions
  • Course descriptions for credit transfer evaluation
  • Completion of at least one academic year at previous institution
  • No disciplinary issues at previous institution
Credit Transfer Guidelines:
  • Maximum of 50% of total credits may be transferred
  • Only grades of C or better are transferable
  • General education credits are more easily transferable
  • Major-specific courses evaluated by department
  • Transfer credits do not affect GGU GPA calculation

Transfer Application

Transfer applicants must submit:

  • GGU Application Form
  • Official transcripts
  • Course descriptions/syllabi
  • Transfer clearance form
  • Recommendation letter
  • Personal statement

Deadline: Same as regular admissions

International Students

Admission Requirements

International students must meet all regular admission requirements plus:

  • Equivalent academic qualifications recognized by NCHE
  • Official transcripts (translated if not in English)
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS) if applicable
  • Valid passport copy
  • Financial support documentation
  • Medical clearance certificate
English Proficiency Requirements:
  • TOEFL: Minimum 550 (paper) / 79 (internet)
  • IELTS: Minimum 6.0 overall
  • Waiver available for English-medium education

Visa & Immigration

Admitted international students receive:

  • Official admission letter for visa application
  • Support for student visa processing
  • Pre-arrival orientation information
  • Airport pickup service (on request)
  • On-campus accommodation assistance

Fraudulent Admission Policy

Zero Tolerance Policy

Grand Gedeh University maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against fraudulent admissions practices.

Prohibited Activities:
  • Submission of forged or altered documents
  • Misrepresentation of academic qualifications
  • Use of fraudulent recommendation letters
  • Impersonation during application process
  • Bribery or corruption attempts
Consequences:
  • Immediate rejection of application
  • Blacklisting from future applications
  • Legal action where applicable
  • Notification to relevant authorities
  • Revocation of admission if discovered later

Reporting Fraud: Suspected fraudulent activities can be reported to admissions@ggu.edu.lr or +231 88 123 4572.
