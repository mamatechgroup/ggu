Our Location

Getting Here

By Road

From Monrovia: Take Robertsfield Highway to Zwedru. The university is located 5km from the city center.

Public Transport

Regular buses and taxis available from Zwedru city center to the university campus.

By Air

Nearest airport: Zwedru Airport (15km from campus). Taxis available from airport to campus.

Visitor Information
  • Parking available at main gate
  • Visitor passes required at security
  • Guided tours available by appointment
