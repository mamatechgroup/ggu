Ensuring excellence in education through systematic quality enhancement

Our Commitment to Quality

The Quality Assurance Directorate at Grand Gedeh University is committed to maintaining and enhancing the quality of all academic programs and institutional processes. Our quality assurance system is designed to ensure that GGU meets national standards while striving for international excellence.

We work collaboratively with all academic and administrative units to implement quality enhancement initiatives that support our mission of providing transformative education.

Academic Oversight

Program Development & Review

We oversee the development, approval, and periodic review of all academic programs to ensure they meet quality standards and remain relevant to societal needs.

  • New program proposal evaluation
  • Curriculum development guidelines
  • Five-year program review cycle
  • External peer review processes
  • Alignment with national qualifications framework

Teaching & Learning Quality

We monitor and enhance the quality of teaching and learning through various assessment mechanisms and faculty development programs.

  • Classroom observation and feedback
  • Student evaluation of teaching
  • Teaching portfolio development
  • Pedagogical training workshops
  • Learning outcome assessment

Performance Evaluation Processes

Institutional Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of university performance against strategic objectives and quality standards.

  • Annual performance reports
  • Key performance indicators
  • Benchmarking studies

Faculty Evaluation

Systematic assessment of faculty performance in teaching, research, and service.

  • Annual performance reviews

Student Assessment

Monitoring student learning outcomes and educational experience quality.

  • Employer satisfaction surveys
  • Student feedback mechanisms
