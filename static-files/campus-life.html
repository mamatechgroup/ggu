Student Support Services

Comprehensive support services ensure student success and wellbeing

Academic Support

  • Tutoring Center: Free tutoring in all major subjects
  • Writing Center: Assistance with academic writing
  • Study Skills Workshops: Time management and study techniques
  • Academic Advising: Personalized course planning
  • Library Services: Research assistance and resources

Health & Wellness

  • Campus Health Center: Medical services and consultations
  • Counseling Services: Mental health support
  • Wellness Programs: Stress management workshops
  • Sports Facilities: Gym, sports fields, and fitness classes
  • Nutrition Services: Healthy eating guidance

Career Services

  • Career Counseling: Resume writing and interview preparation
  • Job Fairs: Annual career fairs with employers
  • Internship Placement: Industry connections and opportunities
  • Alumni Networking: Connections with successful graduates
  • Professional Development: Workshops and seminars

Student Housing

GGU offers comfortable and secure on-campus housing options:

  • Residence Halls: Separate halls for male and female students
  • Room Types: Single, double, and triple occupancy rooms
  • Amenities: Wi-Fi, study lounges, laundry facilities
  • Security: 24/7 security and CCTV surveillance
  • Resident Assistants: Trained student staff for support

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Various financial support options available:

  • Need-Based Scholarships: For students with financial constraints
  • Merit Scholarships: Based on academic achievement
  • Government Sponsorships: Through Ministry of Education
  • Work-Study Program: Campus employment opportunities
  • External Scholarships: Partnership with donor organizations
