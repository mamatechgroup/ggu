Our History

Grand Gedeh University (GGU) stands as a proud testament to the commitment of the Government of Liberia to decentralize higher education in the various regions of Liberia including the Southeast where the institution is located.

Its foundation traces back to the establishment of the Grand Gedeh Community College (GGCC) as a tertiary institution in Zwedru, Grand Gedeh County through the Act of the National Legislature in 2011 and officially opened its doors to students in January 2013.

In a significant move to enhance higher education in the upper-Southeastern region, the National Legislature, recognizing the college's growth and the increasing demand for degree-granting programs, voted to repeal the 2011 Act in July of 2025 to officially grant the institution a full university status.

The elevation from Community College to Grand Gedeh University—a four-year degree-granting institution—marks a new chapter in its history. This transition ensures that GGU continues to fulfill its core mission by offering comprehensive bachelor's degree programs, thus creating greater opportunities for educational advancement, human resource capacity building, and socio-economic development across the entire county and Liberia.

Historical Timeline

  • 2011 - Grand Gedeh Community College established by Act of National Legislature
  • Jan 2013 - Officially opened its doors to students
  • 2013-2025 - Celebrated seven graduation convocations, producing 591 graduates (415 men and 176 women), including 39 Registered Nurses
  • July 2025 - Upgraded to Grand Gedeh University by Act of National Legislature

Our Guiding Principles

Vision

To be a preeminent center of learning globally recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, innovative research, and the development of compassionate leaders who drive positive, sustainable change within their communities and beyond.

Mission

Grand Gedeh University is dedicated to providing an accessible, high-quality education that empowers students to become creative, innovative, and competent professionals. Our mission is to:

  • Educate: Deliver rigorous academic programs that foster critical thinking and practical skills
  • Innovate: Promote a culture of entrepreneurial thinking and research
  • Serve: Instill a deep sense of service and social responsibility

Objective

The Objective of the University shall be to advance Knowledge, Wisdom and Understanding by teaching and research with the ultimate purpose of service to the community.
