Specialized Research Centers

Grand Gedeh University's Centers and Institutes serve as hubs for interdisciplinary research, community engagement, and specialized training. These units complement our academic programs by addressing critical societal challenges and promoting innovation.

Our centers work closely with local communities, government agencies, and international partners to develop solutions that are both locally relevant and globally informed.

Center for Gender Studies

Director: Dr. Aminata Johnson

Mission & Focus

The Center for Gender Studies promotes gender equality and women's empowerment through research, advocacy, and education. We address gender-based issues in Liberian society and contribute to national gender policy development.

Key Programs:
  • Gender Research Program - Studies on gender dynamics in post-conflict Liberia
  • Women's Leadership Development - Training for aspiring female leaders
  • Gender Policy Advocacy - Engagement with policymakers
  • Community Outreach - Gender sensitization programs
  • Academic Courses - Gender studies curriculum
  • Research Publications - Scholarly outputs on gender issues
Recent Projects:
Women in Agriculture Initiative

Empowering women farmers through training and resources

Gender-Based Violence Research

Study on prevalence and prevention strategies

Center for Entrepreneur Studies

Director: Mr. Joseph Cooper

Fostering Innovation & Enterprise

The Center for Entrepreneur Studies promotes entrepreneurship and innovation among students, alumni, and community members. We provide training, mentorship, and resources to help turn ideas into viable businesses.

Programs & Initiatives:
Startup Incubator

Support for early-stage businesses with workspace, mentorship, and seed funding

Entrepreneurship Training

Workshops and courses on business planning, marketing, and finance

Innovation Challenges
